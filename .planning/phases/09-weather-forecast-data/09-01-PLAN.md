   - `_from_evcc(solar: SolarForecast) -> SolarForecastMultiDay` — converts EVCC 15-min timeseries to hourly by summing 4 consecutive slots, uses tomorrow_energy_wh and day_after_energy_wh for daily totals
   - `_seasonal_solar_fallback() -> SolarForecastMultiDay` — uses month-based seasonal averages: summer ~40kWh/day, winter ~10kWh/day, shoulder ~25kWh/day. Distribute across daytime hours (6-20 UTC, Gaussian-like shape), nighttime = 0.
   - `async def get_solar_forecast(evcc_client: EvccClient, weather_client: OpenMeteoClient | None) -> SolarForecastMultiDay` — cascade: try EVCC, then Open-Meteo, then seasonal
