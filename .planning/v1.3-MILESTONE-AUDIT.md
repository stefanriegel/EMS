### Silent Degradation (2/28)

**MISS-01: FeaturePipeline not injected (INFRA-02)**
- `backend/feature_pipeline.py` exists with full implementation
- `ConsumptionForecaster.__init__` accepts `feature_pipeline` parameter
- `main.py` never constructs or injects it
- Fallback: direct `HaStatisticsReader.read_entity_hourly()` calls (works but misses 1h cache and InfluxDB augmentation)

**MISS-02: weather_client not injected (FCST-01)**
- `OpenMeteoClient.get_temperature_forecast()` exists
- `ConsumptionForecaster.__init__` accepts `weather_client` parameter
- `main.py` constructs OpenMeteoClient but passes it only to WeatherScheduler, not ConsumptionForecaster
- Fallback: `self._last_outdoor_temp` cached scalar from training data (stale temperature in predictions)

### Fix
Both gaps are in `main.py` at the `ConsumptionForecaster` constructor call. Fix requires:
1. Construct `FeaturePipeline` before forecaster, pass as `feature_pipeline=`
2. Reorder `OpenMeteoClient` construction before forecaster, pass as `weather_client=`
